Who are we?

We are the 12th group of the Tech Heroes program. Since we started this project our goal was to empower Kosovo's youth. We wanted to create a platform that offers the easiest way to get informed about different kind of youth related activities and by that it would be easier for young people to attend on those activities and to stay active! So we created ActiveYouth platform, that helps young people to find the best extracurricular activities based on their interests.
