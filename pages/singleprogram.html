Active Youth Platform Health

Active Youth Health has a mission to engrave the idea that sports are not about
medals. It is about personal glory and growth and being a part of a likeminded team
and a united community.

Integration

We work a lot in the field of integration of refugees and other disadvantaged groups in the future.

Learning & Sharing

We always seek to learn something new from partners all over the world, share good practices and expand our circle of good-doers.

Be Active
